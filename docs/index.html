1. Problem

  • Work still lives across browser, desktop, email, docs, and chat.
  • Current assistants are chat-first and brittle on real actions.
  • Teams need something that can act and prove what happened.

2. Solution

  • Natural language in, real computer work out.
  • Planner, skill registry, integrations, and actuator in one loop.
  • Every destructive action passes approval and sandbox gates.
